About

What

This project uses location data from my Apple Watch, weather data from OpenWeatherMap.org, AWS DynamoDB, and a Raspberry Pi to retrieve, store, and present air quality and weather data based on my location. Over time, enough data will be collected to analyze for trends in AQI and temperature, and new features may be added in the future to account for average exposure to air pollutants.

Why

Rather than tracking weather in a specific location, this app is tracking weather for a specific person. The data collected over time may be used to see trends in air quality and evaluate how changes in air quality might relate to my wellbeing. Additional data over time will provied opportunities for additional analysis.
